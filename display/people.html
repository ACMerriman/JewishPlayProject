Ignaz Bing

1840 - 1918
Company: Gebruder Bing
Toys: toy trains
A wide line of mechanical and non-mechanical toys
Ignaz founded Gebruder Bing with his brother Adolf initially as a kitchenware manufacturer. In the late 1800s, they began making toy trains. They refined the "Nuremberg Process" of manufacturing 3D toys out of enameled sheet metal parts that were assembled via a tab and slot process. Their successes with the process and their getting on the Teddy Bear bandwagon made them the top toy manufacturer in the world in the early 1900's.Ignaz was a nature buff and discovered the "Bing Cave" in Streitberg in 1905.
